Chocolate Chip Cookie

Fun and easy to make. Prep time: 1h

THE BEST! Recipe for warm, gooey, chocolatey goodness! HMM!


Ingredients:

  • flour
  • eggs
  • milk
  • baking powder
  • baking soda
  • sugar
  • brown sugar

Steps:

  1. Set over to 350 degrees
  2. Mix dry stuff together
  3. Mix liquid stuff together
  4. Pour liquid mix into dry mix...and Mix some more
  5. Add however many choclate chips
  6. Put cookie dough into pan
  7. Bake for 20mins. And enjoy! :D
