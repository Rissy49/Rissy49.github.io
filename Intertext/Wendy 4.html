Intertext


Peter Pan was the lost boy who never grew
up. He lived in Neverland. With him lived his
band of Lost Boys and the pixie, Tinker Bell.
Peter heard Wendy's Wish and flew to her
window.
Wendy and the boys told Peter their
plan. " Ofcourse I'll take you back to
Neverland," said Peter Pan. "Then you won't
ever have to grow up! Just a bit of pixie
dust and off we go!"
