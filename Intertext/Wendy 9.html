Intertext


As Wendy and Peter sat by the beach, a
rowboat came up to shore. In the boat were
Captain Hook and his faithful pirate, Smee!
They climed out of the boat. They crept
behind the bushes. there they could hear
what Peter and Wendy were saying.
"Birthday parties are important, Peter. At ours we had
wonderful cakes. There were presents for
everyone." they heard Wendy say. "I like the
sound of birthday parties, Smee," said
Captain Hook. "I want her to give a birthday
party for me!"
