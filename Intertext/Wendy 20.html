Intertext

Peter Pan was sorry to see Wendy and
the boys go. But Tinker Bell sprinkled
them all with pixie dust so they could
not change their minds. Then she and
Peter flew them home.
Wendy tucked her sleepy brothers into
bed. Then she went to the window.
"Goodbye, Peter Pan and Tinker Bell,"
she said. "Someday we will see you
again. Even though we grow up, part
of us will never really grow up all
the way."
