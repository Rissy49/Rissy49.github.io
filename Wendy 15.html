"Captain Hook wants a paty" said Peter.
"So we'll just give him one. Didn't
Wendy say that sometimes people wear
costumes to parties?" he asked. "Yes"
said Michael. "Silly costumes or
scary costumes."
"Then that is what we'll do. There is
no time to waste. We'll all switch
clothes and wear twigs and branches.
That will surprse Hook and Smee all
right!" said Peter. They all changed
quicly. "Come on everyone. Let's get
them!" cried Peter.
