"We never have birthday parties here,"
said Peter. "We don't grow older." "No
birthday parties gasped Wendy, John, and
Michael. "No, said Peter. "But we make up
for that. We have fun every day for the year!"
"That's right!" shouted the Lost
Boys. "We get up at any time we like-"
"No one tells us to wash our hands or
comb our hair-" All the boys talked at
once. John and Michael cheered up right
away. But Wendy did not look happy.
